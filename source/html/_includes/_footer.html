For the past few years I have re-designed my site every year.

I use it as an opportunity to take stock of where I'm at, and experiment with new ideas.

The 2017 version is built on Uno and with Based by Cabe Branson. The font is Work Sans by Wei Huang.

Design consulting and inspiration from all of the talented people at Cinco Design but mostly Zach, Jared, Cabe, Nate and Spencer and Scott.

When I run into server problems or tricky Javascript I ask Cabe or Nate.

Special thanks to my wife Lauren for user testing, design feedback, and putting up with me.
